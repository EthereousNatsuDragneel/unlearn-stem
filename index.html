The Approach

Why "Unlearn"?

Because most students have been taught to chase marks, not understanding. Before we can build something solid, we have to let go of the shortcut habits — the formula plugging, the rote copying, the panic cramming.

Then we rebuild from the ground up. Stronger, deeper. A student who truly understands the why behind Grade 10 physics will sail through Grade 12. That's the whole idea.

01

Foundation First

We never skip the basics. Every advanced concept is only as strong as what it stands on. We take the time to make the base rock-solid.

02

Teach to Fish

I don't hand out answers. I work alongside students until they can solve problems independently — that confidence sticks long after class ends.

03

Real-World Anchors

Abstract formulas become obvious when you can see them in the world. Every lesson ties theory to something tangible you can hold onto.

04

Personalized Pace

No two students learn the same way. Sessions are adapted to how you think — not pushed through a fixed script.
